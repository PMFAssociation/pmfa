About Us

The Progressive Malayalam Filmmakers' Association (PMFA) is an inclusive collective that aims to represent and support all professionals within the Malayalam film industry. From directors and producers to technicians, artists, and support staff, we embrace the diverse talents that contribute to the richness of our cinema.

Our association is founded on the principles of equality, fairness, and mutual respect. We strive to create a platform where every voice is heard, regardless of background or position. By fostering collaboration and advocating for the rights and well-being of all industry members, we aim to build a more equitable and innovative film community.

Together, we work towards elevating Malayalam cinema on the global stage while ensuring that our industry's growth is inclusive, ethical, and sustainable.

Our Mission

Ethical Responsibility

Ensure all filmmaking practices are rooted in ethical values, promoting fairness and integrity.

Systemic Modernization

Modernize industry operations by implementing transparent frameworks and global best practices.

Workforce Empowerment

Empower all individuals in the industry by protecting their rights and fostering growth.
