About Us

The Progressive Malayalam Filmmakers' Association (PMFA) is a collective of visionary producers, directors, and technicians dedicated to revolutionizing the Malayalam film industry. We recognize the immense potential of our cinema and are committed to fostering an environment that nurtures creativity, upholds ethical practices, and embraces technological advancements.

Our association serves as a platform for collaboration, innovation, and advocacy, ensuring that Malayalam cinema continues to thrive and make its mark on the global stage.

Our Mission

Ethical Responsibility

Ensure all filmmaking practices are rooted in ethical values, promoting fairness, integrity, and accountability in every production.

Systemic Modernization

Modernize industry operations by implementing transparent legal frameworks, standardizing contracts, and adopting global best practices.

Workforce Empowerment

Empower all individuals in the industry by protecting their rights and providing access to professional development programs and resources.

Key Objectives

Equality and Social Justice

Create an inclusive environment free from discrimination, ensuring equal opportunity for all members of the industry, including mentorship programs for underrepresented voices.

Sustainability and Innovation

Encourage sustainable practices in film production and foster innovation through the adoption of new technologies, creative storytelling methods, and modern distribution platforms.
